About Financier

Financier is a fork from Daily Money, and also merge the version from LancelotLiu. Daily Money is a simple & easy financial application. It is also a Google code open source project. you could find the project/source here : Financier (YES, It is OPEN and FREE to use)

Special thanks to Lancelot who contributes code and helps on the Facebook.

Features:

  • Record your daily expense, income, asset and liability.
  • Count balance of money.
  • Export/Import to CSV.
  • Export Database to SD and restore when new installation.
  • Balance pie chart & time chart.
  • Password protection.
